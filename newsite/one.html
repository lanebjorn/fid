This is a painting of a level in the Japanese game Dōkutsu Monogatari, or Cave Story. A game released in 2004. The level is called "The Balcony"

The game is about a reconnaissance robot named Quote, sent to a massive floating island to find a group of missing scientists and their research. The story eventually reveals that Fuyuhiko Date, a character called The Doctor, is the main villain. He was a part of the original research team who visited the island, but he found a magical artifact in the form of a red crystal. With that crystal, it allowed him to control the island.

The Balcony is the final level before the 1st final boss, The Doctor. It contains a slow, calm background track with minimal enemies and several healing items.

What makes this one of my favorite paintings is the beauty of the color and the music that inspired it. The sky is a mix of deep purple and yellow, and I was at first hesitant to mix the two, only for it to create a delightful gradient. I made a few mistakes, but I am very proud of my shadows and color variation.
